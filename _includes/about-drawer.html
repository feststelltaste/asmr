About this Roadmap

This roadmap tracks tools, techniques, and patterns for modernizing software using agentic AI — from AI coding assistants and autonomous agents to legacy migration techniques that get a revival because of the new way we program with LLMs. It uses the six-stage model from cards42's technology roadmap, extending the ThoughtWorks Technology Radar with explicit Stop and Remove stages to cover the full technology lifecycle.
